Why Programming?

It all started when I was 16, in my last year of mandatory studies. There, I chose to study Informatics, and I started to learn about the office IT (PowerPoint, Word, Excel...). But, at the end of the course, teacher taught us some web development, and I started to feel curious about the subject.

Why Programming?

At this moment, I had very clear what I wanted to work in the future, and it wasn't a programmer. I still didn't know how I was going to like this career in the future... And I didn't even think about become a programmer. Here you can see a picture of my very first web I developed. It was only some html pages (without css) with links between them.

My Stack

The programming languages I know are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JAVA, PHP, Bash, MySQL and GIT. The frameworks I have seen are Laravel and Node. I also have studied AWS and Azure. I feel myself more confident as a backend developer, but I would like to develop my skills in order to become a full-stack developer, and that's what I'm looking to turn myself into.

My Stack

Projects

For the moment, I don't have many projects, because I'm starting working as a web developer, but you can see my very first two projects. I hope to add more new ones in the future.

FIRESPOTTERS

This project consists of a predictor of wildfires' movement in real time. It uses 2 APIs to guess where a wildfire will develop and it shows it on the map with colorful cones. This was a project I made in 2023 NASA SpaceApps hackathon event and it won the data use prize and the best project.

PARCHIS

This was my last project in my first course of programming. It consists of a parchis game that can be played online by two players.

Este jueves por la mañana estuve en el Valencia Digital Summit, para poder ver qué novedades hay en el mundo de la tecnología y acercarme al sector IT de manera más tangible, puesto que este mundo se caracteriza porque todo es desde tu ordenador. Pude ver muchos stands con startups que ofrecen propuestas realmente interesantes, además de enterarme de hacia dónde va enfocada la evolución de la industria para los próximos años.
